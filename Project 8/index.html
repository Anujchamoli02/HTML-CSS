About MBA

The MBA is a popular 2-year professional degree course that provides a broad spectrum of students to choose their career field. The MBA admission process starts with the MBA entrance exam and is followed by GD / PI. Students must have more than 50% in graduation from any stream to appear in the entrance examination. MBA Admission 2021 MBA Entrance Exam Top MBA Specialization The full form of MBA is Master of Business Administration and the minimum MBA eligibility requirement for MBA admission is 50% in undergraduates (5% discount is available in some MBA colleges) however top MBA colleges in India require minimum 60% for MBA admission it occurs. Full time (2 year MBA) is the most popular type of MBA followed by students in India. The MBA syllabus is the most market-oriented as compared to other masters courses. MBA subjects include Operations Management, Marketing Management, Supply Chain Management, Strategic Management and more.As the full form of MBA suggests, all types of MBA courses are advanced business degrees designed to meet the ever-increasing demand in the corporate sector. The MBA prepares students for top managerial positions in a company, with core courses in subjects such as accounting, economics, marketing, operations, as well as elective courses that allow participants to follow their personal or professional interests .

Why Study For MBA ?

  • Develop management skills: The MBA curriculum is the same for almost all B-schools and focuses on improving leadership and people management skills; Development, advertising and sales of products and services; Create a connection or partnership; Management of difficult situations; Keeping the company's finances healthy; Gather and create reports based on industry data; Hire top talent and improve employee retention, but also take strict action when needed.
  • MBA Specializations: The sheer number of MBA specializations helps students to focus their attention and study about the industry in which they are interested. An MBA in Digital Marketing is a trending specialization in MBA.   • Network Building: MBA students get an opportunity to dive into the vast sea of ​​alumni networks present in their respective colleges. The connections will help students achieve their dream companies and deepen their understanding of the slight changes in the business environment and discover new ways to adapt faster.
  • High Salary: It is common knowledge that MBA offers the best salary in the current market. It is in high demand and is the most market friendly course available.
  • Opportunity to become an entrepreneur: With appropriate market knowledge and ideas, as well as an MBA degree, students can start their own business. No other course helps students to start their own business.

    • IIM Ahmedabad - Indian Institute of Management

    Ranked 1 by the NIRF 2020, IIM Ahmedabad (IIM A) is considered as the best institution for Management Education in India. Established in the year 1961, it is a public and autonomous institute and one of the few institutes in India to be given EQUIS (European Quality Improvement System) accreditation. This institute is known for its two-year, full-time residential flagship PGP (also referred to as MBA) programme that offers admission based on a valid score in CAT. The percentile of CAT for IIM Ahmedabad reached up to 99.6 in the past years which shows how rigorous it is for a student to get admission to this institute.

    IIM Bangalore - Indian Institute of Management, Karnataka

    Indian Institute of Management (IIM), Bangalore is among the top graduate schools for Management education in Asia. Established in the year 1973, it admits over 1,200 students across several programmes every year. Accredited by EQUIS since 2019, it is the only Indian Management B-school in the Global Network for Advanced Management, a Yale-initiated network of 32 top-notch global B-schools. In the recently released NIRF 2020 ranking, IIM Bangalore was ranked 2 among the top 100 B-schools in India. Located at Bannerghatta Road, Bengaluru, it is in proximity to some of the leading corporate houses in the country thus maintaining a great track record of placements with every passing year.

    IIM Calcutta - Indian Institute of Management

    Established In 1961, IIM Calcutta was the first institute established under the IIM legacy. Ranked 3 under the Management category by NIRF 2020, IIM Calcutta is considered as one of the best B-schools in the country for Management education. The institute offers PG, doctoral and executive programmes to the students out of which MBA (or PGPM) is the flagship course. Popularly known as IIMC, the Institute has consistently seen a good number of recruiters and students who have served in reputed firms across the world.
